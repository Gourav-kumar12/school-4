About Madhavi School

Chairman: Mr. Eknath Baburao Madhavi

Secretary: Mr.Krutarth Eknath Madhavi

Treasurer: Mrs.Sushma Eknath Madhavi

Late Shri Baburao Raghunath Madhavi, the founder of Madhavi School in the year 2000 envisioned an educational institution that would incorporate in its pedagogy a synthesis of modern technology of Teaching and Learning with Indian Values Welcome with immense pride and great pleasure to our web portal. You could be one among the others who is interested to know more about our functioning and the college environment. There was a time when English education was out of reach for the financially backward section of the society. At that time, a noble idea came to the mind of my father, our Baba Shri Baburao Raghunath Madhavi, and he formed a trust in the name of our Guru, known as Shri Sadguru Waman Baba Maharaj Shikshan Prasarak Mandal in the year 1980 with a noble aim to spread English education to financially poor sections of the society at a reasonable fee without accepting any kind of donations and grants. A small initiative of our Baba has now grown as a banyan tree which provides quality education from PG to PG (from Play Group to Post Graduation). The social and cultural values provided to our children are well rewarded with their excellent success in the past years and all our investments and initiatives have resulted in remarkable achievements and helped our children to reach greater heights. I express my sincere thanks to Principal, Teachers and Staff members without whom this success would not have been possible. They not only teach the students, but inculcate high values of commitment.
