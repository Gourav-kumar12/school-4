All that you need to know about Quizzical - Aviation Management

HAL Management Academy (HMA) is the management institute of Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL), a premier aeronautical complex in Asia. As a part of the initiatives to develop the academia-industry interaction, a corporate and management conclave, SAADHANA, with a number of exciting events on Unstop and a final showdown scheduled on the 20th and 21st of January 2023 at HMA are planned.

This event is part of the challenging and exhilarating series of online competitions being conducted as part of SAADHANA'23.

This is your opportunity to be a Maverick, and participate in Quizzical-Aviation Management.

Put your knowledge and Aviation management skills to the test.

The quiz is open to B-school and Engineering students who are currently pursuing their respective programs.

All members of the team should be from the same institution.

The number of participants per team can be a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 4.

Any number of teams can participate from one institute.

No change in team structure will be entertained after the registration of the team.

One participant should only be in one team for this event.

The decisions of the organizers of the contest will be final and binding on all participants.

The organizers reserve the right to any changes to the rules and regulations.

The structure and rules of the quiz, in addition to the above basic rules, will be at the discretion of the organizer.

In case of a tie, the respective teams' quiz completion time will be the deciding factor.
